Winvest

The dashboard tracks key business metrics such as sales, revenues, margins, and key finance ratios such as Gross Margin %, Operating Margin %, and more. The dashboard is built on the 3 statement model which includes the cash flow statement, PnL statement, and Balance Sheets all comparable to previous periods. Each statement is broken down by the use of waterfall charts and snakey diagrams to gain deeper insights. The dashboard is built keeping functionality, UI/UX, and user in mind. The principles laid forward by IBCS have also been followed.
