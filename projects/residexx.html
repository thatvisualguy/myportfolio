Glide Project

Residexx

This Glide app was developed to help a client manage their real estate portfolio. It features four pages, enabling the client to lease properties, monitor tenant status, log property repairs, and manage contractors efficiently. The app incorporates multiple custom actions, enhancing user interaction and workflow automation. With Airtable as the backend, data is seamlessly managed and updated, allowing the client to maintain an organized and up-to-date portfolio.This comprehensive app streamlines property management, allowing the client to efficiently oversee leasing, repairs, and contractor coordination, all within a single, organized platform.
