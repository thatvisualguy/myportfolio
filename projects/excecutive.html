Excel Project

Executive Dashboard

This dashboard is made in Excel using various formulas to extract and present the data in a user-friendly manner along with relevant charts such as pie, column, line, waterfall, line, treemaps, macros, and tabular views. It provides a comprehensive and interactive view of the key performance indicators (KPIs) such as customer satisfaction, quality, productivity, efficiency, profitability, and growth. It also allows users to drill down into the details of each KPI and compare them across different dimensions. It also enables users to make data-driven decisions and implement continuous improvement initiatives based on the feedback and insights from the dashboard.
