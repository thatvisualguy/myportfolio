Power BI Project

Wheels 4 all

This Power BI project was designed for a car dealership chain to streamline their inventory management. The dashboard features three screens: an Overview page, a Car Showroom page, and a Car Inventory page. The Overview page highlights business KPIs, parameters, and charts, including demographic data to track performance. The Car Showroom page displays each car in the inventory with detailed data for easy tracking. The Car Inventory page lists all available cars along with their features, enabling quick access to vital information. This dashboard provides an intuitive solution for managing car inventory and dealership operations efficiently.
