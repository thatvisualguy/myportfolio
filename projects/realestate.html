Tableau Project

Real Estate Analysis

This Tableau project focuses on real estate analysis, providing insights into key metrics such as average house prices, updated daily and broken down by region. The dashboard features statistical visuals, including histograms to visualize the distribution of house prices, bedrooms, and bathrooms. Additionally, a heatmap is used to analyze the relationship between house views, condition, and average price. A dynamic filter pane allows users to explore and customize the data interactively, enhancing insights and decision-making. This comprehensive dashboard empowers real estate professionals with detailed analytics to track market trends and optimize property management strategies.
